# Store Listing & ASO Kit

## App identity
- **Name:** BabyStages
- **Subtitle (iOS, 30 chars):** "What to expect, age by age"
- **Bundle/App ID:** `app.babystages`
- **Category:** Health & Fitness (primary) or Medical; secondary Lifestyle.
  *Do not* use Kids/Designed-for-Families (it's a parent app).

## ASO keywords (seed list)
baby milestones, child development, newborn, infant tracker, parenting, baby
tracker, developmental milestones, toddler, baby growth, what to expect, baby
schedule, milestone reminders, pediatrician, baby app for new parents.

## Short description / promotional text
"See what to expect from your baby at every age — milestones and parenting
guidance from trusted, science-backed sources (CDC, AAP, WHO). Track progress,
get reminders, and support your child's development with confidence."

## Long description (outline)
1. Hook: every age explained, instantly, from your child's birthday.
2. The sections: Developmental Window, Upcoming Milestones, Dos & Don'ts, Daily
   Rhythms, Play Ideas, Safety, When to Talk to Your Doctor, Milestone Tracker.
3. Trust: evidence-based, cited sources; corrected age for preemies.
4. Family: multiple children, family overview, partner sharing (Premium).
5. Premium: unlimited children, reminders, history, PDF for pediatrician visits.
6. Disclaimer: informational only, not medical advice.

## Required URLs
- Privacy Policy URL (host `docs/PRIVACY_POLICY.md`).
- Terms / EULA URL (host `docs/TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md`).
- Support URL + support email. Marketing URL (optional).
- Google Play **account-deletion URL** (web page describing in-app + email path).

- **App icon:** 1024×1024 (no alpha for iOS). Use the 🍼 brand mark / wordmark on
  brand color `#df5e4b`.
- **Screenshots** (use the real, verified screens):
  1. Developmental Window (hero with age + stage progress)
  2. Upcoming Milestones with prep tips
  3. Milestone Tracker with progress
  4. Family Overview (multi-child)
  5. Dos & Don'ts / Safety
  6. Premium paywall
  - iOS: 6.7" + 6.5" + (iPad if supported). Android: phone + 7"/10" tablet.
- **Feature graphic** (Google Play 1024×500).
- Optional app preview video (15–30s).

## In-app purchase metadata
- Products: `premium_monthly`, `premium_annual`, `premium_lifetime` (mirror IDs in
  App Store Connect, Play Console, and RevenueCat).
- Localized display names, descriptions, and prices per market.
- 7-day free trial on subscriptions.
