# BabyStages Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-28
**Contact:** privacy@babystages.app

> Template — review with counsel and host at a public URL before store submission.

BabyStages ("we", "us") provides general, evidence-based child-development
information to parents and caregivers. This policy explains what we collect, why,
and your choices. The app is intended for adults (18+); it is not directed to
children.

## 1. Information we collect
- **Account:** email address and a password (stored only as a bcrypt hash).
- **Child profiles you create:** name, birthday, optional sex, optional avatar/
  photo, premature/due-date flag, and the milestones you check off. You enter and
  control this; we treat it as personal data.
- **Subscription status:** whether you have an active Premium entitlement
  (received from Apple/Google via our payments provider). We do **not** receive
  your card details.
- **Technical/diagnostic:** limited error and performance logs (e.g. via Sentry)
  to keep the app reliable. We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs.

## 2. How we use information
- Provide and personalize the service (age-appropriate guidance, family sync).
- Manage your account and subscription.
- Send essential account emails (e.g. password reset, verification).
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, and fix bugs.

We do **not** sell or share your personal information, and we do **not** use it
for advertising or to build profiles of children.

## 3. Affiliate links
Some product suggestions are affiliate links. If you purchase through them, we may
earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Selecting a link takes you to a
third-party retailer with its own privacy practices, which we don't control.

## 4. Payments
Subscriptions are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play under their
terms and privacy policies. We receive only your entitlement status.

## 5. Sharing with service providers
We use vetted processors to operate the app: cloud hosting, database, transactional
email, payments/entitlements (RevenueCat), and error monitoring. They process data
only on our instructions under data-processing agreements.

## 6. Data retention & deletion
We retain your data while your account is active. You can **export** your data or
**permanently delete** your account anytime in the app (Account screen) or by
emailing us; deletion erases your profile and all child/milestone data.

## 7. Security
Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and protected at rest; passwords are hashed;
access is restricted. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow industry
practices.

## 8. Your rights
Depending on your location (e.g. GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to
access, correct, export, or delete your data, and to object/restrict processing.
We honor these for all users. Contact privacy@babystages.app.

## 9. International transfers
We may process data in other countries using appropriate safeguards (e.g. SCCs).

## 10. Children
The app is for parents/caregivers, not for use by children, and is not directed to
children. Information about a child is provided and controlled by the responsible
adult.

## 11. Changes
We may update this policy and will revise the effective date; material changes
will be highlighted in-app.

## 12. Contact
Questions or requests: **privacy@babystages.app**.
